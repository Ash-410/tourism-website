Bangalore - Garden City

The tech hub of India with a perfect blend of tradition and modernity

About Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital of Karnataka state. Known for its pleasant climate throughout the year, the city is a vibrant cosmopolitan hub that beautifully blends its colonial past with cutting-edge technology and innovation.

Home to numerous parks, lakes, and gardens, Bangalore earned the nickname "Garden City." It's also India's leading IT exporter, hosting numerous tech giants, startups, and research institutions, giving it another moniker: "Silicon Valley of India."

Mysore - City of Palaces

A royal city known for its grand heritage, silk sarees, and sandalwood

About Mysore

Mysore (or Mysuru) is Karnataka's cultural capital and the second-largest city in the state. Once the capital of the Wodeyar dynasty, the city retains a regal charm with magnificent palaces, heritage buildings, and well-planned gardens.

Famous for the grand Mysore Palace, Dasara celebrations, silk sarees, sandalwood products, and traditional Mysore Pak sweet, the city offers visitors a deep dive into Karnataka's rich cultural heritage.
